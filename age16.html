Where I Was Living Then

Nothing in particular about my living situation at this point since I was still in the same ol’ house.

My vivid memories from that age

Me and my friends took AP classes and tests for the first time. I remember that during the whole school year I would take great notes and I’d share them with people who wouldn't take notes in exchange for favors. I’d even help a lot of these people to study for the final as well. I made a bunch of friends like this, so after our finals I remember going around and hanging out all summer long.

The people, places, or objects that mattered to me

In the aforementioned AP class, my teacher would occasionally hold a trivia competition regarding the class materials. I won and the prize was a small unicorn sticker—I still have it.

What I was worried about or excited about

It probably wouldn’t be extraordinary to guess that I was ripping hair out while studying for my AP tests.
