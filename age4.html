Where I Was Living Then

Orange County, California USA. Again, I moved here when I was three, and we started our life in the states in a humble apartment.

My vivid memories from that age

During preschool, I had the most kindhearted teacher whose face I strangely remember vividly; she had hair like sheep’s wool and steel oval glasses. She was OLD old, so sadly I doubt she’s still around today, but I would love to see her again. She gave me a pillow once.

The people, places, or objects that mattered to me

Since my English wasn’t the best, making friends wasn’t easy. However, I met one close friend during preschool that I would reunite with during middle school, which was pretty cool.

What I was worried or excited about

I was probably worried about trying to fit in at school since my English wasn’t great. I was probably really excited about playing games on my parents’ phones.
